HKU 2026 Summer Workshop on Statistics and Data Analytics

This workshop is organized by the Statistics Group within the Innovation and Information Management Area of the HKU Business School.

We aim to bring together leading researchers in statistics and data science from around the world. We focus on promoting global and regional integration, fostering in-person interactions, and discussing cutting-edge research topics. We hope this workshop offers an open platform for participants to share insights in a collaborative environment to the advancement of statistics and data science.

Starting from 2026, the workshop will be held over two days. On the second day, we will co-host the Asia satellite meeting of the IMS International New Researcher Conference (INRC), in collaboration with the corresponding IMS organizing committee.

Morning and afternoon sessions are located in the Jockey Club Tower, Centennial Campus, HKU. The Centennial Campus is an expansion of HKU main campus, providing an inspiring learning and communicating environment.
